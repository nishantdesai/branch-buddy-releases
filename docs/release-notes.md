## 1.0.0 (Beta 8) – 30 Nov 2025 {#1-0-0-beta-8-30-nov-2025}

### New

- New menu bar icon + refreshed app icon.
- Brand-new onboarding flow walks people through finding the menu bar icon, adding repositories, choosing launch apps and customizing the experience. You can relaunch the welcome flow anytime from **Preferences → General → Show Welcome Screen…**

## 1.0.0 (Beta 7) – 24 Nov 2025 {#1-0-0-beta-7-24-nov-2025}

### New

- Monitor unlimited Git repositories with branch, ahead/behind, and dirty-state indicators directly from the menu bar.
- Launch repositories with your preferred apps (Xcode, Cursor, Terminal, etc.), with smart workspace detection.
- GitHub CLI integration surfaces open pull requests and lets you jump straight to viewing or creating PRs.
- Adjustable refresh cadence plus manual “Refresh” and Sparkle-powered “Check for Updates…” actions keep data current on demand.
- Manage repository lists from a streamlined Preferences pane.
- Configure per-user application shortcuts (Xcode, Cursor, Terminal, etc.) to open projects exactly where you want.
- Switch between icon/text status-bar presentations to match your menu bar style.
- Toggle “Launch at Login” to keep Branch Buddy running whenever macOS starts.

### Improvements & Fixes

- Lightweight refresh pipeline keeps the menu responsive even when scanning dozens of repos.
- Hardened runtime, notarization, and Sparkle-powered updates ensure secure distribution for this first public beta.
